# Contributing to AuthForge

Thanks for your interest! We're building in public and would love your help.

## Current Status

We're in the early stages. The best way to contribute right now:

1. **Star the repo** - Shows support and helps others find us
2. **Join Discord** - Where active development discussions happen
3. **Share your use case** - What auth challenges are you facing?
4. **Spread the word** - Tweet, blog, tell other developers
